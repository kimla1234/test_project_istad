# The meaning of Cybersecurity

Cyber Security is the practice of safeguarding systems, servers, computers, mobile devices, cloud infrastructure, networks, and data from various potential threats that can compromise their integrity. It involves protecting against unauthorized access, alterations, or destruction of valuable information. This field aims to prevent security breaches, data breaches, or any activities that may lead to the unauthorized acquisition of sensitive information or disrupt normal operations. Cyber Security plays a crucial role in ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data, and it often involves measures to defend against sophisticated attacks on digital systems.

# why Cyber Security ?

Cybersecurity is a critical and rapidly evolving field that focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from various cyber threats. Several factors contribute to the significance of cybersecurity: - Increasing Cyber Threats
- Data Protection
- Privacy Concerns
- Financial Implications
- National Security
- Global Connectivity
- Technological Advancements
- Regulatory Compliance
